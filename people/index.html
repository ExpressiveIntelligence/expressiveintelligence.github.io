--- title: People ---

EIS accepts new graduate students every year. Former members of EIS are now in a wide range of roles. They are faculty at institutions such as American University, Cal Poly Pomona, Georgia Tech, Goldsmiths, Pomona College, Worcester Polytechnic Institute, UC Davis, UC Santa Cruz, University of Central Florida, and University of New Orleans. They are in industry at companies such as BBN, Caledonia, Electronic Arts, Funomena, Google, Kabam, Spirit AI, Trion Worlds, Y Combinator Research, and Zynga.
